A unique coin depicting a Knorr Viking ship at sea.

Coin 1 crown was issued from August 1, 1934 to March 25, 1941, during the first period of Estonia's independence.

On the obverse of the coin in the center is a large state seal, the emblem of Estonia, crowned with an arched text with the inscription "Eesti Vabariik", and on the lower edge - the year of issue "1934". The reverse depicts a Viking ship Knarr in the sea, under which appears the inscription 1 crown.
